Kick It Out!

You choose from zero to five blocks randomly. Then the computer chooses zero to five blocks randomly as well. These actions are repeated until there aren't any more blocks The one which chooses the last block will be the winner. As you choose a block, your score is increased by one. If you want to finish your turn, press "Done".
